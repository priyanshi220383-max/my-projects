Titan

Titan Company Limited is a leading Indian watch and lifestyle company founded in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). Headquartered in Bengaluru, it transformed the Indian watch industry with stylish, affordable, and high-quality timepieces, introducing modern quartz technology and making watches a fashion accessory for Indian consumers. Titan operates popular brands such as Fastrack and Sonata, and is renowned for its jewellery brand Tanishq. Recognised for reliability, elegant designs, and innovation in both analog and smartwatches, Titan remains one of India's most respected brands.

TIMEX

Timex Group is a well-known American watch company founded in 1854 in Waterbury, Connecticut. Famous for producing durable, affordable, and reliable watches for everyday use, Timex gained worldwide recognition with its slogan "Takes a licking and keeps on ticking." The company introduced innovations such as the Indiglo night-light feature and easy-to-read dial designs. Worn by soldiers, athletes, and everyday people around the world, Timex is considered one of the most trusted and popular watch brands globally.
