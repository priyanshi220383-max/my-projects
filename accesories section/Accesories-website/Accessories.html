Titan

Titan Company Limited is a leading Indian watch and lifestyle company founded in 1984 as a joint venture between the Tata Group and the Tamil Nadu Industrial Development Corporation (TIDCO). It is headquartered in Bengaluru, India, and became famous for transforming the Indian watch industry with stylish, affordable, and high-quality timepieces. Titan introduced modern quartz technology and made watches a fashion accessory for Indian consumers. The company operates popular brands such as Titan, Fastrack, and Sonata and is also known for its strong presence in jewelry through Tanishq. Titan watches are recognized for their reliability, elegant designs, and innovation in both analog and smartwatches. Over the years, Titan has earned the trust of millions of customers and is regarded as one of India's most respected and successful watch brands.

TIMEX

Timex Group is a well-known American watch company founded in 1854 in Waterbury, Connecticut. It became famous for producing durable, affordable, and reliable watches for everyday use. Timex gained worldwide recognition with its slogan "Takes a licking and keeps on ticking," highlighting the toughness of its timepieces. The company introduced several innovations such as the Indiglo night-light feature and easy-to-read dial designs. Timex watches are known for their simple style, long-lasting performance, and accessibility to a wide range of customers. Over the years, the brand has been worn by soldiers, athletes, and ordinary people around the world. Today, Timex is considered one of the most trusted and popular watch brands globally.
